# Finance Committee Analytics & Sponsor Platform

A full-stack web platform built to manage, analyze, and optimize
the financial ecosystem of a college Finance Committee.

This system helps the Finance Committee:
- Track sponsors and partnerships
- Manage event budgets
- Analyze ROI and footfall
- Generate sponsor proposals
- Produce finance reports

It also provides:
- A public portal for transparency
- A sponsor portal for ROI tracking
- An internal FC dashboard for operations

---

- Sponsor CRM
